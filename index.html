Karupatti Coffee is a traditional South Indian beverage sweetened with karupatti, or palm jaggery, instead of refined sugar. Known for its rich, caramel-like flavor, karupatti is made from the sap of palm trees and is packed with essential minerals like iron and calcium. The coffee is brewed as a strong decoction, with the karupatti melted and mixed in to enhance the flavor. Often enjoyed with or without milk, it offers a healthier alternative to sugary drinks due to its lower glycemic index, making it a great option for managing blood sugar levels. Karupatti also boosts digestion and provides sustained energy without the crash associated with refined sugar. Particularly popular in Tamil Nadu, Karupatti Coffee has gained renewed appreciation as people seek more natural and nutritious alternatives. Its unique balance of bold coffee and subtle sweetness makes it both a delicious and wholesome choice for coffee lovers.
